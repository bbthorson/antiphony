## Where the line is

Vox Pop Core is infrastructure, not a finished app. It gives you identity, prompts, replies, and audio behind one API; the product experience — and the higher-level, product-specific features a given app layers on (telephony, hosted widget distribution, teams, billing) — belongs to whatever you build on top. [voxpop.com](https://voxpop.com) is one such app.

Curious what the end-user side of an app built on the core looks like? The [Vox Pop help center](https://voxpop.app/help) walks through recording prompts, managing replies, and sharing embeds.
